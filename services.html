Value-Added Services

Valstom Energy provides value-added services that complement our integrated project package supply.

Our services are focused on readiness for installation, compliance, and operational reliability, ensuring supplied equipment meets project, site, and regulatory requirement before delivery. We support valves and flow-control systems across Oil & Gas, Petrochemical, Power, Water, and process industries through controlled integration, testing, modification, and quality support.

Valve Automation & Integration

We provide complete valve automation and integration services to deliver fully assembled, tested, and ready-to-install valve packages.

  • Assembly of valves with Pneumatic, Hydraulic, and Electric actuators and Gearboxes
  • ON–OFF and Modulating Valve Automation Systems
  • Control valves integrated with smart positioners (HART & Foundation Fieldbus protocols)
  • ESD valves with Partial Stroke Testing (PST) capability
  • Integration of automation accessories including solenoids, limit switches, and positioners
  • Mounting bracket and coupling design & fabrication
  • Control panel / enclosure assembly and setup

All assemblies are aligned with project specifications and supplied with supporting documentation.

Testing & Validation

To ensure performance, integrity, and compliance, Valstom Energy carries out testing prior to dispatch as part of our quality control process. Testing is performed in accordance with applicable standards and project requirements.

Testing Scope Includes

  • Hydrostatic Pressure Testing
  • PMI (Positive Material Identification) Testing
  • Functional Testing (Open / Close Operation under Zero DP)
  • Pre-Dispatch Inspection & Verification
  • Test Records, Reports & Documentation

The testing scope is defined based on project specifications, client requirements, and applicable standards.

Valve Modification & Machining

We support project-specific valve modifications to meet application, service, and specification requirements.

  • Trim changes and End Connection modifications
  • Stem extensions for cryogenic, buried, or elevated installations
  • Pup piece and nipple fabrication as per project requirements
  • Packing and gasket material changes
  • Custom machining for dimensional and specification compliance
  • Valve Tag Plate installation

All modifications are carried out with full traceability, inspection, and documentation control.
